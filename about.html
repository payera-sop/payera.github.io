Our Story

Paa-yera Inspired by the Bengali word for pigeon — a quiet messenger of peace. Through every city and every new beginning, a pigeon by the window always reminded me of home. Paa-yera candles are born from that feeling — a soft flame, a familiar scent, a moment of stillness wherever you are.

Why we choose natural materials

All candles are hand-poured in small batches using soy wax, cotton wicks, and carefully chosen fragrance blends. Our focus is on a cleaner burn, longer-lasting fragrance, and sustainable choices.

Sustainable Soy Wax

Soy wax is renewable, biodegradable, and gives a gentle, even burn.

Pure Cotton Wicks

Lead-free cotton wicks for safety and a clean burn.

Responsible Craftsmanship

Small-batch production preserves quality and attention to detail.

Safety &care

  • Trim wick to ¼ inch before each burn
  • Place on heat-resistant surface away from drafts
  • Never leave a burning candle unattended
