Our Story

Why We Choose These Materials — Quality, Safety & Sustainability

At Payera, every element of our candles is chosen thoughtfully. We want you to enjoy beautiful, long-lasting light without compromise. Here's a closer look at what goes into each jar:

Sustainable Soy Wax

We use 100% natural soy wax, derived from renewable soybeans. Compared to traditional paraffin, soy wax offers several advantages:

  • Cleaner burn — minimal soot when wicked and trimmed properly
  • Longer-lasting — burns slower and more evenly
  • Eco-friendly — biodegradable and renewable
  • Excellent scent throw — holds fragrance beautifully

Pure Cotton Wicks

100% natural cotton (lead-free, zinc-free) for safety and clean burning.

Responsible Burning & Fire Safety

Follow these guidelines for safe enjoyment:

  • Trim wick to ¼ inch (6 mm) before each use
  • Never leave burning candle unattended
  • Keep away from flammable objects (min. 30 cm)
  • Place on heat-resistant surface, out of reach of children/pets
  • Avoid drafts
